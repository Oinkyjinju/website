Connect

Client : B.F.A Thesis 2019
Role :UX & Concept & Retail Store Designer
Target Audience : Babyboomers
Description :Connect creates a bridge between baby boom and smart products to show the positive side in use of smart product in their daily life and benefiting their helath care needs for a long term goals by experiencing and purchasing smart product in creative environment.

Question

" How might UX design be used to help baby boomer selected and use VI enabled smart products that can most benefit to their specific home environtment and health care needs?"

Project objectives

The long-term objective of Connect project can be measured through an increase in the purchase of a smart product by baby boomers. Specifically, measurement of purchase in health care and home environment smart products that assist baby boomers' with their daily routines. The short-term success of this project is measured by increase in recognition and acclimation of smart product by baby boom. Creating intimacy with different demographics can help grow the brand and allows more user to experience the Connect. In the purpose of teaching the benefits of using the smart products for their health care and home environment needs.

Competitive Analysis
The current market of the voice interface based smart product isn't quite bit nor historical as the other digital product industry. Connect's competitors are Smarthome, HoneyCo, and Best Buy. This three had the most similar concept and ideas for target audience.
HoneyCo is the only company focusing on a specific target audience. Such as aging aduts and family for care giving needs. Whereas, Smarthome and Best Buy focus on a middle-class family who wants to improve their house quality through both VI smart product and non-VI interfaced smart home devices.

Smarthome and Best Buy is a franchised retail store with assorted smart devices for a quick and simple search. Brand are limited and not all the employees are informed about the products they are selling. These two companies provide a product under particular categories (i.e., Lightning, thermostats, security, door lock, more home control) Where the product is specifically offer to improve the house quality.

Meanwhile, HoneyCo puts a value in the needs of helping the uses' care giving routines and easy communications through VI based smart products.

User Survey - Findings
Based on the result of a survey, I found that baby boomers were highly interested in new digital devices regrdless of the gender and product brand. 80% of their weekly spending were used in food, 9% on entertainment. The most interesting fact was that only 20% of the interviewee had a concern about their health issue.

Following up with with health care concern; the average age of interviewers are 57. Another interesting result was that baby boomers' experience with a voice interface is zero. Yet they knew what Alexa and Google Home Assistance.

Purpose of Offline Store
From the survey, one of the results showed that the system needs to be improved in recognition and context to understand what types of benefits a user can experienceby using a smart product. Increasing the recognition and understanding of benefits in using smart product is truly hrd to manifest as a short-term goal.

Therefore, the soution of the Connect project focuses on developing an environment that can build the prime context in benefits of smart products to baby boomers. As a result, the solution is to develp an indpendent section in a franchised retail environment Connect can increase the recognition of smart product from the baby boomer based on their purchase behavior.

Baby boomer invets more attention towards the information of the product than the inventory, which leads them to be in a phsycial store environment to experience and learn the product. In additional resutlt, the commonly known franchise retail store builds trust, existing communication, and easy access to information. The "Tryout Room" can build a home environment to provide a comfortable experience while user is experiencing the product. Which may deliver positive outcome in product experience.
